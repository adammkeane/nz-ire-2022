In July 2022, Ireland played a three-Test series against New Zealand, as part of the 2022 mid-year rugby union internationals.
This was Ireland's first tour since their tour to Australia in 2018; their first tour to New Zealand since 2012; and their first encounter against New Zealand since November 2021. This website offers a recap of the 2022 tour.
